Paper Guidelines

The paper must be in English, limited to 6 pages including references, and follow the IEEE conference format. The template is available on the conference website: https://edu.ieee.org/. Submissions are managed via Easychair and will undergo a plagiarism check. The manuscript should not include embedded links, scanned images, headers, or footers.

About the Conference

This conference serves as a comprehensive platform to explore the latest in wireless and mobile communication, focusing on 5G, reconfigurable networks, microwave and millimeter-wave engineering. It highlights sustainable research in signal and image processing, antenna design, RF propagation, and electromagnetic computation.

×

About the Institution

Sri Krishna College of Engineering and Technology (SKCET), established in 1998, is a premier autonomous institution with over 6500+ students, known for experiential education through learning, discovery, and innovation. Accredited by NAAC with an 'A++' Grade, SKCET ranks 83rd in NIRF 2024 among engineering colleges in India.

×

About the Department

Established in 1998, the ECE Department nurtures engineers with strong academic foundations, ethics, and leadership. Offering B.E. and M.E. programs, it is NBA-accredited and recognized as a research center by Anna University, producing 24 Ph.D. scholars.
