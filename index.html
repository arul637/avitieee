Manuscript Preparation

  • Papers must present original, unpublished research and should not be under review elsewhere.
  • Manuscripts must be written in English and formatted according to the conference-prescribed template.
  • The recommended paper length is 6-8 pages (including figures, tables, and references).
  • Authors must ensure clarity, technical accuracy, and proper organization of the manuscript.
×

Paper Submission

  • All papers must be submitted through the online submission system.
  • Submissions must be in PDF format.
  • The paper should be prepared for double-blind review; therefore, author names, affiliations, and acknowledgments must be removed from the manuscript.
  • At least one author of each accepted paper must register and present the paper at the conference.
×

Plagiarism Policy

  • All submitted papers will be screened using plagiarism detection software.
  • The similarity index must be within acceptable limits (typically below 15-20%), excluding references.
  • Papers found to contain plagiarism, self-plagiarism, or fabricated data will be rejected immediately.
×

Ethical Statement

IC3AID 2026 is committed to maintaining the highest standards of research integrity and publication ethics.

  • Authors must ensure that their research is conducted in an ethical and responsible manner.
  • Any use of human participants, personal data, or sensitive information must comply with applicable ethical standards and regulations.
  • Authors must properly acknowledge all sources and avoid data manipulation, falsification, or misrepresentation.
  • Any conflict of interest that could influence the research outcomes must be clearly disclosed.
  • Submissions violating ethical norms will be rejected and may be reported to the authors' institutions.
×

Review Policy

  • IC3AID 2026 follows a double-blind peer review process.
  • Each submission will be reviewed by at least two independent experts from the Technical Program Committee.
  • Papers will be evaluated based on:
    1. Originality and novelty
    2. Technical quality and correctness
    3. Relevance to conference themes
    4. Clarity of presentation
    5. Significance of results
  • The final decision will be made by the Program Chairs based on reviewers' recommendations.
  • Reviewers' comments will be shared with authors for improvement.
×

Publication Policy

  • Only accepted and presented papers will be included in the conference proceedings.
  • Selected high-quality papers may be invited for extended versions in indexed journals, subject to additional review.

Call for Paper

    The rapid evolution of digital technologies has led to an unprecedented growth in data generated from intelligent systems, connected devices, and digital platforms. This transformation has intensified the need for advanced computational paradigms capable of learning, reasoning, and supporting intelligent decision-making. In this context, Cognitive Computing, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Data Intelligence have emerged as key research domains driving innovation in computer science and engineering.

    Cognitive computing aims to emulate human cognitive processes by enabling systems to perceive, interpret, and respond to complex information in a contextual and adaptive manner. When integrated with AI techniques such as machine learning, deep learning, and natural language processing, these systems demonstrate enhanced capabilities in handling unstructured data and solving real-world problems. Concurrently, data intelligence plays a crucial role in extracting meaningful insights from large-scale and heterogeneous datasets through advanced analytics, data mining, and visualization techniques.

    The convergence of cognitive computing, AI, and data intelligence is fundamental to the development of intelligent applications across domains including healthcare, smart cities, finance, education, and industrial automation. However, challenges related to scalability, explainability, security, and ethical deployment of AI systems remain significant. Addressing these challenges requires interdisciplinary research and global collaboration. The ICÂ³AID 2026 conference provides an international platform to advance research, share knowledge, and promote sustainable, human-centric intelligent technologies.

    IC3AID 2026 invites original, unpublished, and high-quality research papers from researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students in the areas of Cognitive Computing, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Intelligence.

    The conference aims to provide an international platform for sharing innovative ideas, research findings, and technological advancements that shape intelligent and data-driven systems for the future.
